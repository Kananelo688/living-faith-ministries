Children's Christmas Party

21 Dec 2024

On 21st December 2024, Living Faith International Ministries hosted a heartwarming Children’s Christmas Party, bringing together young hearts for a day filled with joy, laughter, and the true spirit of Christmas.

See More...

What a joyful and heartwarming celebration it was! On a magical December day, our church family came together to host an unforgettable Christmas party for our little ones. Smiles, laughter, and the spirit of giving filled the air! 🎁💖

What made it Special?

  • ✨ Bible Storytime – Teaching the true meaning of Christmas and the birth of Jesus Christ.
  • 🎶 Christmas Carols & Worship – Children sang their favorite Christmas songs, filling the church with a joyful melody.
  • 🎨 Arts & Crafts – Kids created beautiful Christmas decorations and cards to take home.
  • 🎭 Drama & Skits – A special nativity play performed by the children, retelling the story of Jesus’ birth.
  • 🎁 Gift Giving & Surprises – Every child received a special Christmas gift, spreading love and generosity.
  • 🍰 Delicious Treats – A feast of festive snacks, sweets, and a Christmas cake for everyone to enjoy.

More than just a party, this event was about spreading love, sharing happiness, and creating memories that will last a lifetime. Our children's ministry worked irelessly to ensure that each child felt special and blessed. 🙏❤️

We are grateful to all the parents, volunteers, and church members who made this day possible. Seeing the smiles and excitement on the children’s faces reminded us of Jesus’ words:

"Let the little children come to me, and do not hinder them, for the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these." – Matthew 19:14

Divine Encounter: Crusade with Prophet L.I. Ntilane

14 November 2024

On November 14, 2024, Living Faith International Ministries was honored to host a transformative Crusade led by Prophet L.I. Ntilane, a respected servant of God known for his profound teachings and prophetic ministry.

See More...

The nights like no others! The atmospheres were charged with faith, miracles, and the undeniable power of God. Prophet L.T. Ntilane delivered a life-transforming message, stirring hearts and awakening destinies. Worship echoed through the halls, lifting souls into the presence of God, where breakthroughs and divine encounters unfolded.

Testimonies of healing and restoration flooded the night. Chains were broken, lives were renewed, and the fire of revival ignited the hearts of many. From prophetic declarations to heartfelt prayers, this crusade was a true manifestation of God's supernatural move.

Those who attended left with renewed faith, stronger convictions, and a deeper hunger for God. This was not just an event—it was an encounter that will be remembered for years to come.

The crusade drew a large gathering of believers eager to experience a deeper connection with God. The evening was filled with:

  • 🙏 Anointed Worship: The congregation entered into heartfelt worship, setting a reverent atmosphere.
  • 📖 Prophetic Ministry: Prophet Ntilane delivered a powerful message, emphasizing trust in God's timing and ways.
  • 🔥 Miracles and Healing: Attendees witnessed manifestations of God's power through healing and deliverance, reaffirming faith and hope.

About Prophet L.I. Ntilane:

Prophet L.I. Ntilane is renowned for his dynamic preaching and prophetic insight. He has ministered across various regions, including Botswana, bringing messages of faith and transformation. His commitment to spreading the Gospel has impacted many lives, leading them to a closer walk with God.
